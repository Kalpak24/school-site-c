About Vidyamandir English School

Our history, vision, mission and the values we stand for.

Our History

Founded with a Vision

Vidyamandir English School was established in 1998 by a group of educators who believed that quality English medium education should be accessible to all children in Pune. Starting with just a handful of classrooms, the school has grown into a thriving institution serving hundreds of students across Classes I to X.

Over the decades, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to CBSE curriculum, experienced teaching staff, and an environment where every child feels valued, supported and inspired to reach their potential.

Our name, "Vidyamandir" — meaning Temple of Knowledge — reflects our philosophy that education is sacred and every child deserves the best.

Admission Rules

Admission Process

  • Admission forms are available at the school office during working hours (Mon–Sat, 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM).
  • Filled forms must be submitted with required documents: birth certificate, previous school's transfer certificate, progress report, Aadhaar card copy, and passport-size photographs.
  • Admission is subject to availability of seats and an interaction session with the child and parents.
  • Fee payment must be completed within 7 days of confirmation of admission to secure the seat.
  • Students are expected to maintain regular attendance (minimum 75%) to appear in examinations.
  • School uniform is compulsory from Day 1. Details of uniform are provided at the time of admission.
  • All students must abide by the school's code of conduct, which is shared in the school diary.
  • Parents are requested to attend Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTMs) scheduled throughout the year.
